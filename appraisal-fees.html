What's Included in Our Appraisals?

All of our appraisal reports include:

  • Complete property inspection and measurement
  • Comprehensive market analysis and comparable sales research
  • Three approaches to value: sales comparison, cost approach, and income approach (where applicable)
  • Professional photographs and site plans
  • Detailed narrative explaining valuation methodology
  • IRS-compliant formatting for tax and legal use
  • Digital delivery of final report

How to Get Started

Ordering your appraisal is easy:

  1. Contact us: Call (951) 309-2131 or (888) 844-1733, or fill out our contact form to provide your property details and appraisal type.
  2. Confirm details: We'll review your request, confirm the scope of work, and provide pricing.
  3. Schedule inspection: If an interior inspection is needed, we'll coordinate a convenient time.
  4. Receive report: Your completed appraisal report will be delivered digitally, typically within 3-5 business days.
