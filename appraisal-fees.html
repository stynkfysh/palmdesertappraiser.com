Appraisal Services & Fees

Transparent pricing for estate, divorce, litigation, pre-purchase, and specialized appraisals throughout the Coachella Valley.

Our Appraisal Services

Specialized valuations for every situation, from family transactions to court proceedings.

Date of Death & Step-Up Basis Appraisals

Establish fair market value as of the date of passing for IRS estate settlement and stepped-up tax basis calculations. Reports meet all federal and state requirements for probate and tax reporting.

Divorce & Bankruptcy Appraisals

Court-accepted neutral valuations for family law and bankruptcy proceedings. Over 1,000 divorce appraisals completed with a two-client discount available for shared valuations.

Expert Witness & Litigation Support

Professional testimony for property disputes, tax appeals, and contested valuations. Written reports and court testimony designed to withstand cross-examination and appeal.

Pre-Purchase & Pre-Sale Appraisals

Know the true value before you buy or list. Avoid overpaying, set a realistic asking price, or establish fair market value for any property transaction.

Estate & Trust Appraisals

Probate valuations, trust distribution appraisals, and tax reporting documentation. Reports prepared to satisfy probate courts, trustees, and beneficiaries.

Tax & PMI Removal Appraisals

Property tax appeal appraisals, gift tax valuations, and PMI removal appraisals. Specialized reports for mortgage lenders and tax authorities.

Family Transaction Appraisals

IRS-compliant valuations for family sales, gifts, and inter-family transfers. Independent appraisals that satisfy IRS requirements and avoid valuation disputes.

Insurance Dispute Appraisals

Support for insurance claims and coverage disputes. Detailed valuations that address policy limits and help resolve claim disagreements.

Turnaround Time

Most appraisals are completed within 3–5 business days of the inspection. Rush service is available for court deadlines and time-sensitive transactions — contact us to arrange expedited delivery.

What You Receive

Every appraisal includes a professional written report suitable for courts, attorneys, the IRS, lenders, and financial institutions. Reports are delivered digitally via email and include comprehensive market analysis, property description, and defensible valuations.

Payment Options

We accept payment by credit card, check, or electronic transfer. Payment is due upon completion of the appraisal. Contact us to discuss invoice terms for large appraisal assignments or estate work.
