Real Estate Appraisal Services in Palm Desert, California

As the central hub of the Coachella Valley, Palm Desert is home to a diverse real estate market ranging from established single-family neighborhoods near El Paseo to newer master-planned communities and luxury estates along the hillsides. The city's strong commercial core, excellent schools, and proximity to shopping and dining make it one of the most sought-after residential areas in the valley.

Palm Desert Real Estate Market

Palm Desert's real estate market features everything from affordable condominiums to multi-million-dollar custom homes. Popular neighborhoods include The Lakes, Palm Desert Country Club, Desert Willow, and the communities along Portola Avenue. The median home price tends to sit near the valley-wide average, though hillside properties and gated communities can command significant premiums.

Local Highlights

El Paseo shopping district, Living Desert Zoo and Gardens, Palm Desert Civic Center Park, College of the Desert, proximity to I-10 and Highway 111.
