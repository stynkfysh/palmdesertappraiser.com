Real Estate Appraisal Services in Rancho Mirage, California

Known as the 'Playground of Presidents,' Rancho Mirage is one of the Coachella Valley's most prestigious residential communities. The city is home to world-class country clubs, gated estates, and the renowned Eisenhower Health medical center. Its reputation for exclusivity and privacy attracts high-net-worth homeowners, making accurate appraisals critical for estate planning, trust administration, and luxury property transactions.

Rancho Mirage Real Estate Market

Rancho Mirage real estate ranges from country club villas to sprawling custom estates on oversized lots. Thunderbird Heights, The Springs, Tamarisk, and Mirada Estates are among the most prominent neighborhoods. Property values in Rancho Mirage tend to be among the highest in the valley, with many homes featuring golf course frontage, mountain panoramas, and resort-style amenities.

Local Highlights

The River at Rancho Mirage shopping center, Agua Caliente Casino, Eisenhower Health, Sunnylands Center & Gardens, numerous championship golf courses.
