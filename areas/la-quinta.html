Real Estate Appraisal Services in La Quinta, California

Nestled against the Santa Rosa Mountains, La Quinta is known as the 'Gem of the Desert' and offers some of the Coachella Valley's most scenic residential settings. The city combines a charming Old Town village atmosphere with world-class golf communities and newer master-planned developments. La Quinta's growing population and expanding amenities make it a popular choice for families and retirees alike.

La Quinta Real Estate Market

La Quinta's real estate spans a wide range, from affordable homes in the Cove and newer developments near Jefferson Street to luxury estates in PGA West, Tradition, and La Quinta Country Club. The SilverRock resort area continues to develop, adding new inventory to the market. Mountain-backdrop properties in the Cove area command premium pricing due to their dramatic settings and limited supply.

Local Highlights

La Quinta Resort & Club, PGA West golf courses, Old Town La Quinta, La Quinta Arts Festival, SilverRock Resort, Santa Rosa Mountain hiking trails.
